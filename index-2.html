Extension: applying R2-RD to crypto

Hirsa et al. (2024) demonstrated R2-RD on macroeconomic indicators and futures markets. This tab extends the methodology to Bitcoin — a 24/7, higher-volatility asset where regime instability is more pronounced. Because crypto lacks a VIX equivalent, 30-day realized volatility serves as the fear proxy, testing whether R2-RD generalizes beyond its original domain.

What is market regime detection?

Financial markets cycle through distinct behavioral states — bull expansions, stress crises, transitions, and recoveries. Identifying the current regime in real time lets investors adjust risk exposure before conditions deteriorate. The core challenge: markets are nonstationary, meaning the statistical fingerprint of "stress" in 2008 differs from 2020 or 2022.

The R2-RD innovation (Hirsa, Xu & Malhotra 2024)

Standard Gaussian Mixture Models (GMMs) trained once become stale as markets evolve — they suffer from temporal instability. R2-RD solves this with three mechanisms: (1) adaptive rolling retraining as new data streams in, (2) temporal ensemble voting that smooths label instability across a lookback window, and (3) data-driven model selection that picks the best-fitting latent variable model from a candidate pool rather than assuming one fixed structure.

How RegimeRadar implements it

This tool fetches real daily price and volatility data via Alpha Vantage on every page load. It computes rolling z-scores of VIXY (VIX proxy ETF) and 20-day SPY momentum as the two-dimensional feature space. A rolling classifier with ensemble smoothing (window = 5) assigns regime labels updated daily. The confidence score (0–100) measures label stability across a 10-day trailing ensemble.

Regime confidence score (0–100)

Rather than a binary regime label, RegimeRadar outputs a continuous confidence score. Above 75: stable, well-defined regime. Below 40: transition likely — Hirsa et al. show mutual fund return dispersion widens sharply during these windows, making it the most critical signal for risk management. The score updates with every page load using fresh market data.
